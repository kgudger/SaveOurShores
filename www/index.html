INSTRUCTIONS

Please enter your user name and email at the top.

The app will attempt to automatically locate your clean up site. If it doesn't, please choose one from the drop down list.

Today's date will appear in the Date section. Clicking the date will open a calendar.

Click "+" or "-" to add (or subtract) each trash item as you find them. You can also enter a total number in the field next to the item's name.

The most found items are at the top of the data card. If you don't see a trash item there, scroll down to find it.

New! Voice Recognition

You can enter data with your voice. Click on "Click to speak" and say a number followed by the item name. An example is "3 paper pieces". Please wait a few seconds and you'll see the amount added to the current tally for that item.

IMPORTANT

Please remember to click the Submit button once you have finished your cleanup.

Your total pounds of trash and recyclables collected will automatically update with each submit.
